Best Thing I read lately The Lightning Thief!

The most recent and best thing I read recently is Percy Jackson "The Lightning Thief" The book is set in the modern day world and follows the main character Percy Jackson son of Poseidon, the Greek god of the sea.

Percy embarks on a quest to prove his innocence and prevent a catastrophic war between the gods. He is joined by his friends Annabeth Chase, a daughter of Athena, and Grover Underwood, a satyr. Together, they journey across the United States, encountering various mythological creatures and challenges, while also learning more about Percy's true powers and destiny.

Throughout their quest, Percy and his friends face numerous trials and confrontations with mythological beings, including Medusa and the Minotaur. They also uncover a prophecy that suggests Percy might be the key to resolving the conflict among the gods.

As the story unfolds, Percy must not only confront external threats but also grapple with his identity as a demigod and the challenges that come with it. "The Lightning Thief" is an adventure filled with humour, Greek mythology, and lessons about friendship, bravery, and treachery.
